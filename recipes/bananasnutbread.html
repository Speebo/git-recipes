Banana Nut Bread

Short, sweet, simple...what more could you want?

Ingredients

  • 0.5 cup shortening
  • 1 cup white granulated sugar
  • 3 beaten eggs
  • 3 ripe bananas (smashed)
  • 2 cups sifted all purpose flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp baking soda in 3 tbsp cold water
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup nuts of choice

Steps

  1. Cream shortening and sugar
  2. Add eggs and bananas, mixing well
  3. Add remaining ingredients
  4. Bake in a long greased bread pan at 350Â°F for 1 hour
